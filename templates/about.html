About Attendance Monitor

Attendance Monitor is a web application designed to make tracking student attendance effortless. Upload group photos, process attendance automatically and access summary reports all in one place.

How it works:

  1. Upload group photo of students
  2. Check generated attendance report
  3. View daily attendance records
  4. View total attendance summary
  5. Check the student image database
  6. Download full attendance report
